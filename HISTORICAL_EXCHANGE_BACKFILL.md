# Historical Exchange Backfill

Backfilling historical exchange data requires providing evidence to back up listing and delisting dates. 

## Trust Levels
- **VERIFIED**: Evidence collected from cryptographically verifiable exchange signatures or verifiable archives.
- **UNVERIFIED**: User-provided assumptions, external unverifiable third-party data.

Backfilled data must be paired with snapshots to pass strict RIF promotion. Unverified backfill will inherently limit promotion to exploratory tiers since survivorship bias risks cannot be ruled out safely.
