# PR4B0-R1P5R abandoned-evidence registry

This registry permanently quarantines the non-reproducible R1P5 source, its receipt chain, and checkpoint. The evidence remains preserved and hash-verifiable for forensic use only.

The mandatory dispositions are `SOURCE_COMMIT_NOT_REPRODUCIBLE`, `CHECKPOINT_NOT_RESEARCH_ELIGIBLE`, and `RECEIPT_CHAIN_NOT_REUSABLE`.

No listed raw response, normalized fragment, receipt, cursor, manifest, partition, chain entry, checkpoint, binary, or cached HTTP body may enter R1P5R or satisfy readiness.
